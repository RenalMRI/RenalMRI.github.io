Renal MRI

RenalMRI.com is a work in progress attempting to consolidate current knowledge in the evaluation of renal masses on magnetic resonance imaging (MRI).

Renal Masses

Renal masses are classified into solid versus cystic if there is ≥25% enhancing component.

Solid Renal Masses

Solid renal masses are characterized by the clear cell likelihood score (ccLS)

Cystic Renal Masses

Cystic renal masses are characterized by the Bosniak classification
